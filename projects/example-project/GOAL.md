## Mission

Demonstrate the AGENTLab project structure and conventions.

## Research Questions

1. How to organize shared AI agent context for a research team?
2. How to maintain cross-tool compatibility (Codex + Claude Code)?

## Principles

- **Simplicity** — Plain markdown, minimal tooling
- **Progressive disclosure** — Load context only when needed
- **Two-level architecture** — Lab (shared) vs Personal (local)
